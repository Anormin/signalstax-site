Questions
COMMON
QUESTIONS.
What leagues does Next Tier cover?
We cover 38 leagues including AEHL, AJHL, BCHL, USHL, NAHL, OJHL, NCAA D1/D2/D3, and ACHA. If your player's league isn't listed in the intake form, reach out and we'll add it within 24 hours.
How is the PDS different from a regular scouting report?
A traditional scouting report is a snapshot in time from a single observer. The PDS is a composite score built from verified stats, film analysis, and trajectory data — compared against 4,200+ players. It updates every evaluation cycle, so it reflects current momentum, not a one-day impression.
What does the 48-hour film evaluation include?
After you submit footage (MP4, MOV, YouTube, or Google Drive link), our AI engine produces a full PDS update plus a coachable metric breakdown covering offensive reads, defensive positioning, physical compete, two-way reliability, and puck management. A credentialed scout reviews the AI output before it's delivered.
Can I switch plans or cancel at any time?
Yes. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription at any time from your account dashboard. There are no long-term commitments or cancellation fees. Your player's profile and historical PDS data is retained for 12 months after cancellation.
Is Next Tier available for female hockey players?
Yes. Next Tier covers PWHL, NWHL, ACHA Women's, and all provincial female leagues. Benchmarks and pathway scores are calculated against gender-appropriate comparison groups.
